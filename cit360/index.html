Learning and Teaching:

Sometimes this can be hard to do remotely, but at my work we have meetings everyday where someone is remote, including myself. I think that this is becoming more and more common in the work place. It could be easy to get distracted at home with kids or things that you want to get done around the house, but you have to be disciplined. Just like in this class. You have to be responsible for your own learning and getting your work done.
